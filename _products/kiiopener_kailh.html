This CNC machined switch opener will speed up any Kailh-style switch modifying project. Made out of high durability 6061 aluminum, and tumbled to a smooth finish. These are manufactured by me in Washington State USA.

All orders will ship within 1-2 business days, and will be packaged in a recyclable and biodegradable bubble mailer.

This keyswitch opener will only work on Kailh style housings with two tabs. It will NOT work on Alps or Cherry MX switches, nor will it work on switches already soldered into a board. If you are planning on opening NK creams, please use the mx style opener rather than this one.
